Our Mission

Who We Are &
Why We Do This

The CIA Program was founded on a simple belief: that students who understand consumers — how they think, what they want, and why they buy — are the professionals businesses need most.

We are a student-led organization committed to preparing members for careers in consumer insight, market research, and data analytics. Our leadership team works year-round to deliver meaningful programming, build industry relationships, and create a culture where curious, driven students thrive.

Above all, we believe that insight is only as powerful as the people who deliver it — and we are committed to developing both the technical skills and the professional character our members need to succeed.
