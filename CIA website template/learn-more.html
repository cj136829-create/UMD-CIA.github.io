FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply to CIA?

Sophmores interested the program.

By what date does my application need to be submited?

Deadline is March 1 or whenever all available spots fill up

Do I need to be good at math or with computers?

An interest in problem-solving is helpful, but you’ll learn the necessary technical skills in the program. Courses are designed to build your confidence in analytics, even if you're new to it

Do you need an internship for this major?

Yes, but they are included in the program, along with company projects and more!

Do I need a specific computer or any other forms of technology for the program?

No, you will have 24/7 access to the CIA lab and that is where you will complete projects.
